What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide information to website owners, and enhance user experience.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period, while session cookies are deleted when you close your browser.

How We Use Cookies

24ToolHub uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Deliver personalized advertisements
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve our services based on usage data

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

Here are links to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Google Ads Settings - Control Google ad personalization
  • Digital Advertising Alliance - Opt out of interest-based ads
  • Google Analytics Opt-out - Browser add-on to opt out of GA

Your Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. By clicking "Accept All," you consent to our use of all cookies. By clicking "Reject Non-Essential," you consent only to essential cookies.

You can change your preferences at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site
  • Adjusting your browser's cookie settings
  • Using the opt-out links provided above

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

  • Some features may not work properly
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • You may see more generic advertisements
  • We won't be able to improve our service based on usage data
