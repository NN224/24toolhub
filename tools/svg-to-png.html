Why Convert SVG to PNG?

Compatibility: While SVG is excellent for web use, PNG has wider support across different platforms and applications.

Fixed Dimensions: PNG images have specific pixel dimensions, making them ideal for platforms with size requirements.

Transparency: PNG supports transparency, preserving the alpha channel from your SVG.

Social Media: Many social platforms require PNG or JPG for profile pictures and cover images.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use 2x scale factor for modern displays and retina screens
  • Maintain aspect ratio to prevent distortion
  • For icons, use dimensions like 512x512, 256x256, or 128x128
  • Higher scale factors produce larger file sizes but better quality
