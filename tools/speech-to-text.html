Convert Speech to Text in Real-Time

Our Speech to Text converter uses advanced Web Speech API technology to transcribe your voice into text instantly. Perfect for writers, students, journalists, and anyone who needs to convert spoken words into written content quickly and accurately.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Transcription: See your words appear as you speak
  • Multiple Languages: Support for 13+ languages and dialects
  • High Accuracy: Advanced speech recognition for clear transcription
  • Word & Character Count: Track your content length in real-time
  • Download & Copy: Save your transcription or copy to clipboard

Common Use Cases

  • Dictating emails, documents, and articles
  • Transcribing meetings and interviews
  • Creating voice notes and memos
  • Accessibility for users who prefer voice input
  • Language learning and pronunciation practice

How to Use

1. Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu
2. Click the microphone button to start recording
3. Speak clearly into your device's microphone
4. Click the microphone again to stop recording
5. Download or copy your transcribed text

Browser Compatibility

This tool uses the Web Speech API and works best in modern browsers like Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. Make sure to grant microphone permissions when prompted.
